Our Story

Born in Addis, Built on Beef

SAR Burger was born from a simple love of great food — the kind that makes you close your eyes and savour every bite. We set out to bring real smash burger culture to Addis Ababa, using quality beef, house-made sauces, and buns baked to perfection.

Our name, ሣር (SAR), reflects our roots. Every item on our menu is crafted with intention — from the Naked Smash to the Philly Cheese Steak, each dish tells a story of quality, community, and flavour.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or settling in for a feast with friends, SAR is your spot.
